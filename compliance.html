Compliance Statement

Effective date: 2025-10-10

Anti-Corruption and Anti-Fraud

WESIGN TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED (“We Sign”) maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward bribery, corruption, kickbacks, or improper advantages in any form. We comply with applicable anti-corruption laws and require our employees, agents, and partners to do the same.

  • No offering, giving, soliciting, or receiving any bribe or improper payment, directly or indirectly.
  • No facilitation payments, no disguising of transactions or improper gifts, hospitality, or expenses.
  • Immediate reporting of suspected violations to compliance contacts listed below.

Trade Controls and Export Compliance

We comply with applicable export control, sanctions, and customs regulations. Our products and services may be subject to restrictions under the laws of relevant jurisdictions. Customers agree not to use or resell products for prohibited end-uses or to restricted parties or destinations.

Counterfeit Mitigation and Quality

We implement supplier vetting and quality measures to mitigate counterfeit risk in electronic components. Buyers should follow proper receiving and inspection procedures and notify us promptly if any non-conformance is suspected.

Conflicts of Interest

Employees and representatives must avoid conflicts between personal interests and company interests and must disclose any potential conflicts to management for review.

Whistleblowing

We encourage reporting of suspected misconduct in good faith. We prohibit retaliation against anyone who raises concerns honestly.
